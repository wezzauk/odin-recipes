Rhubard Cordial

Description

A refreshing cordial that takes 10 minutes to prep. Use up a glut of fresh rhubarb and make the most of summer

Ingredients

  • 300g golden caster sugar
  • zest and juice 1 orange
  • zest and juice 1 lemon
  • 450g rhubarb, chopped
  • 1 slice fresh root ginger, peeled

Steps

  1. STEP 1 Put the sugar in a large saucepan with 300ml water. Bring to a simmer then add the zest and juice of both the orange and the lemon along with the rhubarb and the ginger.
  2. STEP 2 Cook the mixture over a medium heat until the rhubarb is falling apart.
  3. STEP 3 Pour the mixture through a sieve lined with muslin into a clean heatproof jug then transfer to sterilised bottles. Keeps in the fridge for up to 1 month.
  4. STEP 4 Serve approx. 25ml of cordial per 100ml sparkling water, or to taste.
